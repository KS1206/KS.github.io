Simple sponge cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raisng flour
    •

Notes on Ingredienets

Castor sugar

Finely granutated white sugar

Self raising flour

A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agents(usually salt and baking powder).

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 degree
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan
  3. In a medium bowl,whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy
  4. Fold in flour
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan
  6. Bake 20 mintutes in the preheated oven until the yop of the cake springs back when lightly pressed
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack
